Residential & Renovation
Not only do I posses skills within the computer science world, but I also have experience in real estate. I do both residential real estate & real estate investments. In terms of residential real estate, I am on track to recieve my real estate license and become an agent for Re/MAX. My growing interest for residential real estate sparked my discovery o real estate investments, more specifically fix & flips. This term is used to describe the process in which investors renovate an old house in order to refinance it and make a handsome profit. After the renovation and refinance, investors proceed to rent out the property to pay off the new mortgage while making a profit, then proceed to use the refinance profit to repeat the process with a new property. I hope to use this strategy to build a sizeable real estate portfolio and bring in constant passive income.
